Cable (Snake) Drain Cleaning Service

Drain cleaning service is not just a matter of manpower; you need the right equipment to unclogg a drain as well. At The Service Professional, we have every drain cleaning machine from small hand snakes to drain machines capable of cleaning big sewer mains. One of our most powerful and flexible machines, however, are the cable drain cleaning machines (also known as a "Drain Snake"). The cable drain cleaning machines clean the drain or sewer by rotating a flexible cable made of piano wire through the pipe. Attachments on the end of the cable aid in the cleaning process by scraping, boring, cutting, and retrieving the pipe blockage. Each type of pipe blockage requires the proper size cable and cutting head. Not just anyone can use these machines to their full drain cleaning potential. It takes experienced, qualified technicians, and The Service Professional has them.

High Pressure Water Jetter- Hydro Jetting-

A water jetter is a specially designed high pressure pump that blasts away strong blockages and clears heavily clogged drains. The Service Professional uses these powerful jetters to clear away whatever is causing the blockage and allow water to drain freely again. Jetters are often the most effective method for clearing drains but aren’t always used when the really should be. So get the job done right, the first time and call The Service Professional today.
